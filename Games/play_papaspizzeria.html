Papa's Pizzeria

Description

A Rated E Game allows players to give people the right order. Help Roy run the pizzeria while Papa Louie is gone. When Papa Louie runs off on another adventure, Delivery Boy Roy is left in charge of Papa's Pizzeria. Unfortunately for Roy, the customers are used to Papa Louie's style of fully custom pizzas.

Controls

  • Use mouse to take orders, cook, etc.
